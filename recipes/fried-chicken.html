Cripsy Fried Chicken

Description

This is the only crispy fried chicken recipe you'll ever need. Why? It combines all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Combine flour, paprika, salt, and pepper in zip-lock bag - shake to combine
  2. Dip chicken in buttermilk, then place in flour mixture
  3. Use deep fryer to heat oil to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  4. Fry chicken until golden brown and no longer pink the center
  5. Drain on paper towel
